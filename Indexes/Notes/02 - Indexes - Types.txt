1. SINGLE COLUMN: 
	1. Indexes that are used against the most frequently used column.
	2. When we satisfy one condition
2. MULTI COLUMN: 
	1. Indexes that are used against most frequently used columns.
	2. When we satisfy multiple conditions.

3.Unique:
	1. For speed and integrity.
	Always use unique keyword.

4. Partial:
	1. Index over a subset of a table.
	
5. Implicit:
	1. Automatically created by:
		a. Database
		b. Primary and Unique Key.
