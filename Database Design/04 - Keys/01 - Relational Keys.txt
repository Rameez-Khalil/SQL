Relation Keys: 
	Uniquely identifies a record in a table.
	When we are going to form a relation, we need to have keys.
	

SUPER KEY: 
	Any combination of attributes that can uniquely identfies a record.
	Combination of email and name, doesn't matter how many.

CANDIDATE KEY: 
	The minimal amount of attributes that can uniquely identifies a record.
	Id and name can be called candidate key.
