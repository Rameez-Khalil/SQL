NORMALIZATION: 
	1. Works on the basis of bottom up. - Existing system.
	2. ERD works on the basis of top-down - New system.
	3. Designing a database with little or no data redundancy.
	

NORMAL FORMS: 
	First Normal Form.
	Second Normal Form.
	Third Normal Form - In most cases this is the finalized normal form.
	
	

FIRST NORMAL FORM: 
	Every column needs to have a single value.
	Each row should be unique, either through a single/multiple column. Not mandatory to have a primary key.
